# Analytics

**URL:** `/dashboard/analytics`

Business intelligence dashboard for message volume, customer growth, and order conversion.

## Metrics overview

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Messages today / week / month | Inbound + outbound WhatsApp messages |
| Total customers | Unique customer phone numbers |
| New customers | First-time messagers in period |
| Orders | Count and revenue |
| Conversion rate | Orders ÷ conversations (approximate) |

## Charts

Typical visualizations (via Recharts):

- **Messages over time** — daily/weekly trend line
- **Orders over time** — revenue and count
- **Top products** — most ordered items
- **Top FAQs** — most triggered FAQ matches

## Date range

Select period filters (7 days, 30 days, custom) to adjust all widgets.

## Growth Engine analytics

For social attribution metrics, use **Dashboard → Growth** instead. That section covers:

- Post → click → WhatsApp → order → revenue funnel
- ROI, cost per lead, ad spend
- Top performing posts

## Plan limits

Message counts tie to subscription **usage limits**. View current usage under [Subscription & Billing](subscription.md).

## Export

Export raw data (orders, customers, chats) via [Import & Export](data-import-export.md) for external analysis in Excel or BI tools.
