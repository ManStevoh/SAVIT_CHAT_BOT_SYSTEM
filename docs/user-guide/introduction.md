# Introduction

## What problem does RelayIQ solve?

Businesses in Africa and globally increasingly sell through WhatsApp, but manually replying to every message does not scale. RelayIQ gives each business:

1. A **connected WhatsApp Business number** with automated replies
2. An **AI assistant** trained on their products, FAQs, and brand tone
3. A **structured order flow** that collects items, quantities, and delivery details
4. **Integrated payments** (M-Pesa STK push, Stripe cards)
5. A **web dashboard** to manage everything in one place
6. Optional **Growth Engine** tools to post on social media and track which posts drive WhatsApp leads and revenue

## How the platform is organized

RelayIQ is a **multi-tenant SaaS** product of **Essem Digital Innovation Limited** ([essemdigital.com](https://essemdigital.com)):

- **One platform** (RelayIQ, powered by Essem Digital Innovation Limited) serves many **companies** (tenants)
- Each company has its own products, FAQs, WhatsApp number, orders, and settings
- **Super admins** manage the platform; **company owners** manage their business
- Customers interact only through **WhatsApp** (and payment links) — they never log into the dashboard

## Subscription plans

Three plans are available (configurable by super admin):

| Plan | Price | Highlights |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Starter** | $29/mo | 1 WhatsApp number, 5,000 messages/mo, basic AI, Growth Engine (20 AI posts, 1 platform) |
| **Growth** | $99/mo | 3 numbers, 50,000 messages/mo, advanced AI, Growth Engine (100 posts, 3 platforms), analytics |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited numbers/messages, 500 Growth posts, 10 platforms, SLA, custom integrations |

All paid plans include a **14-day free trial** (Starter and Growth). Enterprise requires contacting sales.

## Key concepts

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Company / tenant** | A business account on the platform |
| **Chat** | A WhatsApp conversation between a customer and a company |
| **Auto-reply** | Bot-generated responses when no human agent is active |
| **Human mode** | Agent takes over; bot pauses until "hand back to bot" |
| **Attribution link** | Short URL (`/g/{slug}`) that tracks social clicks to WhatsApp |
| **Active subscription** | Required for bot auto-replies and most dashboard features |

## Supported languages & regions

The dashboard UI is in English. Bot replies follow the company's configured greeting and FAQ content. M-Pesa is optimized for Kenya; Stripe supports international card payments.
